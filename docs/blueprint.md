# **App Name**: landing casares

## Core Features:

- Information presentation: Showcase key services (tax preparation, auditing, financial planning) and their value proposition to the user
- Contact form: Allow potential clients to send direct inquiries via a contact form.
- About us section: Introduce the accounting team and their expertise with concise biographies and credentials.
- Testimonial Carousel: Showcase client testimonials to build trust and credibility.
- FAQ Section: Address common questions about accounting services and regulations in Argentina.
- AI Chatbot Assistant: Provide a 24/7 AI chatbot to answer basic inquiries and qualify leads. The tool has information about accounting regulations and policies in Argentina to effectively qualify leads. 

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Deep blue (#1E3A8A) to evoke trust, stability, and professionalism, inspired by the traditional colors associated with finance and government in Argentina.
- Background color: Light gray (#F5F5F5), desaturated from the primary color to create a clean, professional backdrop.
- Accent color: Gold (#D4AF37), to signify prestige, quality, and highlight key calls-to-action.
- Body and headline font: 'Inter' (sans-serif) to offer a modern and approachable reading experience. Suitable for headlines and body text.
- Use clean, professional icons to represent services and key information.
- Design a clear, modern layout with intuitive navigation, optimized for all devices.
- Incorporate subtle animations to enhance user experience and guide attention, particularly when interacting with the chatbot or browsing the testimonial carousel.
